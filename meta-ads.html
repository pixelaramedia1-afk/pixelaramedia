The Process

Campaign Setup & Targeting Methods

  • 1

    Account Audit & Pixel Structuring

    We ensure the Conversions API (CAPI) is firing perfectly, deduplicating data, and sending rich offline conversion data back to Meta. Without pristine tracking, the algorithm cannot optimize efficiently.

  • 2

    Broad & Lookalike Layering

    We utilize broad targeting paired with strict exclusion lists, along with high-value Lookalike Audiences (LALs) built from your top 10% lifetime value customers, to feed the system high-quality signals.

  • 3

    Dynamic Creative Testing (DCT)

    Using a sandbox campaign structure, we rapidly test ad copy, headlines, and creative assets (UGC, statics, motion graphics) to find outlier winners before moving them into the scaling campaigns.

  • 4

    Conversion Optimization Focus

    Campaigns are strictly tied to backend ROI. We focus on Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) and Return on Ad Spend (ROAS). If a campaign looks good on Facebook but isn't translating to bank revenue, it gets cut.
