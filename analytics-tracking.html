In modern advertising, the algorithm is only as good as the data you feed it. We implement robust, server-side tracking infrastructure so you never lose a conversion again.

Data You Can Trust

Since the iOS 14.5 update and the deprecation of third-party cookies, basic tracking pixels are no longer enough. If your ad platforms aren't receiving accurate purchase and lead data, they will optimize blindly, destroying your ROI.

We solve this by building "Single Source of Truth" reporting dashboards and implementing server-side tracking via Google Tag Manager (GTM) and Meta Conversions API (CAPI).

By capturing first-party data and securely passing it back to the ad networks, we restore match rates to 90%+, dramatically lowering your CPA and giving you the confidence to scale your budgets.

The Methodology

Infrastructure Setup Process

  • 1

    GA4 Configuration

    We properly configure Google Analytics 4, setting up custom events, cross-domain tracking, and predictive audiences so you understand exactly how users flow through your site.

  • 2

    GTM Implementation

    Instead of hardcoding messy scripts on your site, we deploy Google Tag Manager. This centralizes all your marketing tags (Meta Pixel, Google Ads Tag, TikTok Pixel) to improve site speed and reliability.

  • 3

    Server-Side Tracking (CAPI)

    To bypass browser restrictions and ad blockers, we implement the Conversions API, sending purchase and lead data directly from your server to the ad platforms.

  • 4

    Custom Reporting Dashboards

    Using tools like Looker Studio, we build custom, automated reporting dashboards that show you your true blended ROAS, CPA, and revenue without needing to log into five different platforms.
